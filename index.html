About

Vegas raised me on late sets and real ears.

Folks describe my voice as soulful — sometimes "angelic," which I'll take. I move between soft indie stuff, R&B and soul, alt-rock grit, and country when the story calls for it. It's all me, one acoustic, and a loop pedal so the room still feels full.

I write my own songs and I know a lot of yours. Coffee shops where you could hear a pin drop, casino lounges where nobody knew my name yet, festivals where the whole field sang back. Same David either way.

"Built loop by loop — honest, loud when it needs to be, quiet when it counts."
