The Artist

Soulful. Versatile. Live.

David Rice is a singer/songwriter in Las Vegas, NV — best known for his angelic soulful voice and his versatility in style, ranging everywhere from soothing indie acoustic sounds to upbeat R&B/soul, alternative rock, and country.

He specializes in acoustic guitar paired with his loop pedal, bringing a full-band experience to every stage. Well-versed with a deep catalog of covers and original music, David has graced stages of every size — from coffee shops and intimate lounges to restaurants, casinos, and music festivals.
