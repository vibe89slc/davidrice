What to include in your email

The more I know up front, the faster I can come back with availability and a number. No need for a formal request form — just hit me with the basics.

  • Date and rough time frame
  • Venue name and city (or if it's at home, just the neighborhood)
  • Type of event — bar, wedding, birthday, corporate, etc.
  • Approximate guest count and whether there's a PA / sound system
  • Any songs you definitely want (or definitely don't want)
  • Your budget range — helps me say yes or find a way to make it work

I'm Las Vegas-based and available most weekends. I travel for the right gig — just ask.
